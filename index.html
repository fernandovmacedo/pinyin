How to use

  • Type a syllable followed by 1, 2, 3, or 4 to add the tone mark
  • Type v to enter ü and V for Ü (type vv for a literal v)
  • Press Enter to accept the current syllable
  • Press Escape to cancel the current syllable
  • Backspace works normally
  • Check Pinyin underlines invalid syllables; turn it off when writing non-Pinyin text
  • A dotted underline marks a missing apostrophe between syllables, such as Xi'an
  • A teal underline marks a tone-sandhi hint — the syllable is pronounced with a different tone than written, such as nǐ hǎo (said ní hǎo)
  • Tone colors tints each syllable by its tone

Example: Type Ni3 hao3! Wo3 jiao4 Li3 Ming2, hen3 gao1xing4 ren4shi ni3. to get Nǐ hǎo! Wǒ jiào Lǐ Míng, hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ. ("Hello! My name is Li Ming, nice to meet you." — all four tones plus the neutral tone.)
