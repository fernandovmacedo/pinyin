# Pitch Perfect Pinyin — Unit Four

## Pronunciation notes for j, q, and x

Editorial summary of the source lesson:

- These initials are followed by an i- or ü-based final.
- With an i-based final, the lips are unrounded; with an ü-based final, they
  are rounded.
- The contrast between j and q includes aspiration: q is pronounced with a
  noticeable release of air, while j is not.
